Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 2 Tomatoes (puree)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 cup Cream
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add spices and mix well.
  4. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add cream and simmer for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with roti or rice.

Tips

Use fresh paneer and butter for best taste. Add a pinch of sugar to balance flavors.
