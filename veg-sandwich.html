Veg Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Cucumber (sliced)
  • Tomato (sliced)
  • Boiled potato (sliced)
  • Green chutney
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Spread butter and chutney on bread slices.
  2. Layer vegetables evenly.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with another bread slice.
  5. Toast or grill if desired.
  6. Serve fresh with ketchup.

Tips

Use fresh bread and add cheese for extra taste.
